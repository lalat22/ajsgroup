WELCOME!

It is a proud moment for us at Sadguru Foundation to unveil our newly designed website! While our unwavering focus is on the elimination of acute poverty among tribal and rural communities through natural resource interventions that are environmentally, technically and socially sound, we aim spread the story we have been weaving for over four decades.

Development is essential and eternal it perpetuates through generations. However, it needs a platform that creates equal opportunities for underserved and underprepared communities to elevate themselves. We wish to create awareness about the plights and needs of some of the poorest and most backward people of our country, India. We want to open up a window to the world on how we can empower them by restoring, developing and expanding the natural resources through development & training programmes and activities.

We look forward to your support and suggestions for improving our initiative and the website.
